MY STORY

Born and raised in New York City, I'm a Full-Stack Developer/Product Manager with a passion for working on useful and innovative products.


After beginning my career in finance, I moved to the technology industry in 2012 as a Product Manager where I managed several mobile and web applications. Having a strong desire to develop a more technical skill set, I enrolled in General Assembly's Web Development Immersive program, where I've learned Ruby, Rails, database management systems, JavaScript, Backbone.js, and other technologies.


I'm a lifelong Giants, Yankees, and Knicks fan, as well as a fairly big nerd, enjoying the (not so) occasional fantasy novel, strategy boardgame, and Star Wars marathon. I often joke with anyone who cares that I'm just as likely to be found playing bball on the NY blacktop as I am re-reading the Lord of the Rings. My sense of humor principally derives from Seinfeld. If you enjoyed that show at some point in your life we will almost certainly get along well (no worries if you haven't, we still probably will).
